Student's Life

Hostels in IIT Indore

Hall of Residence for students at IIT Indore is located in the permanent campus of the institute at Simrol. The hall was inaugurated on 29th November 2016 by Prof. Pradeep Mathur, Director IIT Indore. The newly constructed building of the hall has latest amenities. Each unit has phone, sofa set, fridge and discussion table for the comfortable stay of the residents. Presently, more than 900 students are residing in the hall. Staying here will be a wonderful and memorable experience.

Eateries in IIT Indore

IIT Indore facilitates the students here with providing a variety of options for sumptous meals. The campus has some really good food joints - Aladeens, Taste Buds, Zippy Cafe. A wide variety of South Indian and North Indian dishes are available. These eatouts serve both vegetarian and non vegetarian cuisines, including a wide range of choices.

Also, there is Shiru Cafe, powered by ENRISSION, a private company of Japan. This cafe is built with the concept that "be a place where students can learn about a profesional world and envision their future career".
